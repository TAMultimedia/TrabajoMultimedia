Música de Fondo: "Sat Tee Touy", de Darkie.

Overclocked Reality is a mix between a 3D puzzle horror and a hack-and-slash heavily inspired by the gothic steampunk aesthetics of games such as Clock Tower and Alice: Madness Returns.

Story:

The Fracture, The Forgotten Dimension. Why would you come back here? There isn't much to see here that you haven't seen yet. Well, the Clock King recently returned. And I guess he's trying to open a portal into an alternate reality. And we will all probably die when that happens. It's not like neither of us can do anything about it. Maybe you wanted to wave this world goobye before that happens? Or perhaps you're just feeding your morbid sense of completion. Either way, it's not like I can stop you.

Gameplay:

Overclocked Reality contains a mix of puzzle horror-based gameplay sections and action filled hack-and-slash sections. While traversing The Fracture, you will have nothing but your trusty, comically-oversized needle to defend yourself. While traversing the Overworld, you will be fully unarmed, forced to use a combination of your wit, your survival skills, and the sheer running capacity of your two legs to escape the many horrors coming to hunt you down.
